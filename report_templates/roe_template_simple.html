3. Rules of Engagement and Support Agreement

  • Confidentiality: All information discovered during this engagement will be treated as strictly confidential and will only be shared with the authorized points of contact.
  • Data Handling: The assessor is permitted to exfiltrate data necessary to demonstrate a proof-of-concept for identified vulnerabilities. No sensitive data (e.g., PII, PHI) will be exfiltrated without explicit, written permission from the Client's primary POC.
  • Service Disruption: While testing may be intrusive, the assessor will make every effort to avoid disruption of services. No intentional Denial of Service (DoS) attacks will be performed unless explicitly included in the scope. If a service disruption is suspected, the assessor will immediately halt testing on the affected asset and contact the emergency POC.
  • Third-Party Systems: No testing will be performed on third-party systems or services. If a vulnerability in a third-party system is discovered, the assessor will report it to the Client for their own vendor management process.
  • Incident Discovery: If evidence of a prior or ongoing real-world compromise is discovered, the assessor will immediately pause the engagement and notify the emergency POC.
